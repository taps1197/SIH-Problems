    		Triple Plus Solution, Raipur Robotic assistance for workers to paint the ceilings
    Problem Statement Details
    Description
    The most difficult place to plaster or paint are the ceilings, as the work to do there is against gravity. Worker finds it tedious to work because of the height. Workers need quick mobility with height and reach to the ceiling. So, a solution is suggested which the workers can use and execute the work without much loss in efficiency along with the required mobility around the room area. >> Clarification - The metal extension legs are just an example. You are free to build your own solution - it can be robotic / non-robotic. The criteria are - 1/ Convenience of use - In most cases of construction the people going to use this solution are not very educated (but are smart). They would prefer a robust, hassle free, fast system with quick work results. As the ceiling work is against the gravity, it drains a lot of human energy and tires them out reducing overall efficiency. 2/ Multipurpose - Better if it is made multipurpose, for enabling other building construction functions too - examples - plastering/putty/painting of walls, concrete mixing, floor leveling etc 3/ Cost effectiveness. Even an aam aadmi should be able to buy it, in case it goes for mass production. 4/ Mobile and sturdy.
    Problem Statement Type Central Ministry
